Step 1 of 5

Welcome to VP Chef Relay

This app runs on your PC and connects your IP cameras to the VP Chef Studio mobile app. Setup only takes two minutes.

What this app does:
Runs silently in your system tray, reads live video from your IP cameras over your local WiFi, and sends footage to VP Chef Studio so you can control recordings from your phone.
You'll need:
• Your IP cameras connected to the same WiFi as this PC
• The cameras' RTSP password (check the camera app or label)
• The VP Chef Studio app open on your phone
Step 2 of 5

Find Your Cameras

Tap Scan Network to automatically find cameras on your WiFi — or enter each IP address manually if you already know them.

📖 How to find your camera IP address →
Can't find your cameras? Make sure they're on 2.4 GHz WiFi (not 5 GHz) and powered on. Cameras that can't be reached will still be saved — they'll show as offline until they come online.
Step 3 of 5

Link to Your Account

Paste your Relay Token from VP Chef Studio so your cameras are scoped to your account — not mixed with other users' relays.

Where to find your token:
Open VP Chef Studio → Settings → scroll to Camera Network → copy the token shown under "Your Relay Token".

Leave blank to use the legacy shared relay (not recommended for new setups).

Step 4 of 5

Camera Password

Enter the RTSP password for your cameras. This is usually set during camera setup in the camera's app. If you haven't changed it, try leaving it blank or use the default from the camera label.

💡 For LaView cameras: the password is whatever you set in the LaView app during initial camera setup. It's not your WiFi password.
Step 5 of 5

Almost Done!

Review your settings, then tap Launch Relay. The relay will start in your system tray and connect automatically.
